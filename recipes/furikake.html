Transforming Furikake Rice from Food Wars (Shokugeki no Soma)!

"The transforming furikake rice is a delicious Japanese dish where the eggs transform when the gelatinized cubes of flavor melt.

This dish is found in Food Wars, or Shokugeki no Soma, season 1, episode 2, and is made by Soma.

Preparing this dish will leave your friends amazed and waiting for the recipe.

You will need several locally found ingredients. However if you can't find sake in your local grocery store,

you will have to go to an Asian grocery store.

The sesame oil will give this delicious meal an amazing nutty aroma.

Do not be shy with the ginger and the garlic. The more, the better.

Cook the spring onion with butter before you add it to the eggs during garnishing. This will taste better.

Serve it hot and watch the cubes melt.

Enjoy!"

Ingredients

  • 8 Unbreaded chicken wings, uncooked
  • 4 Eggs
  • 2 Green onions
  • 3 Tbsp of soy sauce
  • 2 Tbsp of sesame oil
  • 2 Tbsp of gelatin
  • 1 Tbsp of sugar
  • 2 tsp of bonito soup stock
  • 2 tsp of minced ginger
  • 5 cups of water
  • 1 cup of white rice
  • 1/4 cup of sake
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Add sesame oil to a pan and heat to medium. Fry the chicken wings for 10-15 minutes, or until golden brown. Save the chicken residue in the pan.
  2. Add water and soup stock to a pot and heat to a boil. Add the chicken and turn the broth down to a medium-low.
  3. Add the ginger, soy sauce, sake, and sugar to the chicken pan. Stir all of these ingredients with a wooden spoon.
  4. Pour everything from the pan into the broth. Simmer on medium-low for 20 minutes.
  5. Take the broth off the heat and let it sit for a few minutes. If there is a layer of fat on top, take it off. Take the chicken wings out and set aside.
  6. Add the gelatine to 1/2 of water and let bloom for 5 minutes. Mix the gelatine into the water until fully dissolved. Add the gelatine mix to the broth mix.
  7. Grease tray with butter. Add the broth. Cover with cling wrap, put in the fridge, and let it solidify for 2 hours.
  8. Separate the chicken from the bones and skin. Add the chicken to the rice, unless you want the recipe to be exactly like Food Wars (Shokugeki no Soma).
  9. Take the gelatin broth out of the fridge and cut into small cubes of broth gelatin.
  10. Scramble eggs. Add eggs and chopped green onions to rice. Add the aspic (meat broth jelly).
  11. Most Important Step: Watch the cubes melt and enjoy!
