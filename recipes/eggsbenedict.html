The Hangover Cure: Eggs Benedict

Ingredients

  • 1 Toasted Muffin
  • 2 Poached Eggs
  • Cooked Ham
  • Hollandaise Sauce

Hollandaise Sauce

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tsp of Chopped Tarragon
  • 1/2 tsp of Water
  • 1/2 tsp of Cyder/White Vinegar
  • 50g Melted Butter
  • Pinch of Salt
  • Pinch of Cayenne
  • Squeeze of Lemon

Instructions

  1. Whisk together the egg yolks, water, salt, and tarragon. After a couple of minutes the mixture should be pale and light. Then whisk for a further two minutes over a bowl of simmering water until the eggs become a little thicker and creamier. Now is a good time to poach your eggs.
  2. Remove from the heat and slowly add the melted butter whilst continuing to whisk until the sauce becomes thick and creamy - you will not need all of the butter and add it slowly to stop it from seperating.
  3. When you're happy with the consistency, add a pinch of cayenne and squeeze of lemon to taste.
  4. Lay the ham on the toasted muffins, add the eggs and ladle over the sauce.

Tip: Marinate yourself in alcohol to get the hangover just right
