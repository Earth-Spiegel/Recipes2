Creamy 4-Ingredient Blueberry Yogurt Cake

"With only 4 ingredients, this recipe is super easy to make at home for this hot summer.

It's soft and creamy with a slightly sweet taste.

Perfect with many fresh fruits like strawberry, orange, pineapple, and peach...my family loves it!"

Ingredients (6-inch mold)

  • 4 Eggs (Medium Size)
  • 4 Tbsp (40g) Cornstarch
  • 2 cups Sweetened Yogurt (if you use plain yogurt, add sugar/honey to your liking)
  • 1/2 cups (~70g) of your favorite fruits (blueberry, strawberry, orange, peach, pineapple)

Instruction

  1. Whisk eggs with yogurt until well combined
  2. Add cornstarch and mix together until smooth (you can strain to make sure the batter has no lumps)
  3. Add fruits and put some on top for garnish (optional)
  4. Oven Method: 340F (170C) for 50-55 mins or until the surface is yellow
  5. Air Fryer Method: 275-300F (135-150C) for 30-35 mins or until the surface is yellow and the cake smells amazing
  6. Let it cool completely. Chill for at least 2 hours before serving
